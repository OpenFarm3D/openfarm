Learn More

The Challenge

Clusters of hardware used for 3D Printing can be unruly, requiring micromanagement of a multitude of details concerning both the hardware and software sides of the equation.

Small-business owners may struggle with the setup and maintenance of their own solution given the aforementioned difficulties.

The Solution

Openfarm is an integrated hardware and software platform designed to abstract away many of the complexities associated with the management of 3D Printers.

In doing so, we free our users from having to worry about any technical problems with their Byzantine complexity, allowing them to focus squarely on their own users.
